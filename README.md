# 🎯 Smart Resume Matcher

An **AI-powered platform** that analyzes your resume and helps you find the perfect job match based on your **skills**, **experience**, and **goals**. Powered by intelligent matching algorithms, resume insights, and a seamless application tracker.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 📄 **Resume Analysis**  
  Upload your resume and get a detailed breakdown of your skills, experience, and qualifications.

- 🤖 **AI-Powered Job Matching**  
  Let our algorithm analyze your resume and recommend the best-fit jobs from multiple sources.

- 🧠 **Skills-Based Matching**  
  Compare your skills with job requirements and discover areas to grow.

- 🔎 **Smart Job Search**  
  Filter job opportunities and view personalized **match scores**.

- 🗂️ **Application Tracker**  
  Keep track of your job applications and their current statuses.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Getting Started

### ✅ For Users

1. Create an account
2. Upload your resume (**PDF format**)
3. Use the **AI Job Matches** feature
4. Apply to jobs directly
5. Track your application status

💡 _The system automatically matches you to relevant job listings using resume parsing + AI._
